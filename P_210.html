TreckTrack (CPSC_210 Course)


Desktop Application – 2025

During my first year at UBC, I took a computer science course on software construction (CPSC_210). This taught me the basics of good software design and how to code in Java. To complete the term, we had to work on a personal project that would apply the concepts learned in class.

I chose to create an application tracking past and future hikes, called TreckTrack.
