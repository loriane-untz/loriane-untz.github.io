Niji


Riddle Game – 2021

In my freshman year of highschool, my friend and I created Niji for a school project. Our assignment was to use the platform Wix to create a riddle game. Players would have to solve various puzzles to obtain passwords, enabling them to move on to the next "stage" of the game. We ended up having so much fun making Niji that we worked on it outside of school in order to refine our creation.

In Niji, you follow a young cat on his journey through a mystical and unknown world to help him find his owner.
